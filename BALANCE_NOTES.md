# Balance Notes for First Closed Test

## Goals
- Give testers 20-30 minutes of stable early gameplay.
- Let them experience:
  - exploration
  - battle
  - capture
  - infection
  - emotional birth

## Current early-game targets
- 8-12 explorations before first forced recovery
- first capture within first 5-10 encounters
- first infection stage visible within first 10-15 minutes
- first emotional birth within 15-25 minutes

## Economy
- starting gold: 120
- starting energy: 12
- monster heal cost: 8 gold
- energy recovery cost: 3 gold

## What testers should report
- too hard to survive
- too hard / easy to capture
- emotional births too frequent / too rare
- not enough gold
- too many empty or low-value explorations
